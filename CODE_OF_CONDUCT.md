# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Godot Auto Combat Kit to be a useful and respectful open-source project. Contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate with care, assume good intent, and focus on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.
- Give clear reproduction steps and technical context.
- Accept maintainer decisions when a contribution is out of scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, close issues, decline pull requests, or block contributors whose behavior harms the project.

To report a concern, contact the maintainer privately through the security contact listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
